What is the ACT Ecolabel?

Learn more

The Accountability, Consistency, and Transparency (ACT) Ecolabel is the premier environmental impact factor label for laboratory products. It provides scientists and procurement professionals with clear, standardized information about the environmental performance of laboratory consumables and equipment.

Accountability

Manufacturers are held accountable for their environmental claims through rigorous third-party verification and transparent reporting.

Consistency

Standardized scoring methodology ensures consistent evaluation across all product categories and manufacturers.

Transparency

Clear, accessible information about environmental impact factors enables informed decision-making.

Environmental Impact Categories

The ACT Ecolabel evaluates products across multiple environmental impact categories, providing a comprehensive assessment of sustainability performance.

Manufacturing

Energy consumption, water usage, and waste generation during production processes.

User Impact

Energy consumption and environmental impact during product use and operation.

End of Life

Disposal considerations, recyclability, and environmental impact at product end-of-life.

Packaging

Materials used in packaging and their environmental impact and recyclability.
